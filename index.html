ABOUT US

' A Photo is worth a Thousand Words ' - said a really smart dude sometime.

Hello and welcome to SwizzleBooks! Snoop Dogg thinks 'swizzle' means 'sweet' and 'fabulous' and so do we!
We are a young startup with a simple yet ambitious mission - to provide easy-to-use tools for students to capture memories of/with their friends. We are committed to providing students with fun and colourful yearbooks in both digital and physical formats. Relive that crazy party, that fun road trip or that exciting college festival as and when you want to!
